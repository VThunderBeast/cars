AUDI
R8: THE V8 ICON
PERFORMANCE MEETS LUXURY
The Audi R8 is the epitome of high-performance engineering and timeless design. Powered by a naturally aspirated 5.2L V10 engine, the R8 delivers exhilarating power with up to 602 horsepower, coupled with Audi's legendary quattro® all-wheel drive system. Its sleek, aerodynamic body is crafted for both speed and elegance, while the luxurious interior offers advanced technology and premium materials. Whether on the track or the open road, the R8 provides a perfect balance of precision, performance, and refinement. Experience the thrill of a true supercar with the Audi R8.

BMW
M5: THE ULTIMATE DRIVING MACHINE
POWER AND PRECISION
The BMW M5 is a masterpiece of engineering, combining the luxury of a sedan with the heart of a high-performance sports car. Under the hood lies a twin-turbocharged 4.4L V8 engine, delivering up to 617 horsepower in the M5 Competition model, ensuring breathtaking acceleration and speed. With advanced M xDrive all-wheel drive and cutting-edge technology, the M5 offers precision handling and unrivaled driving dynamics. Inside, the M5 features a driver-focused cockpit with premium materials and innovative tech, providing comfort and control in every drive. The BMW M5 is designed for those who demand power, luxury, and a thrilling driving experience.
DODGE
CHARGER SRT HELLCAT
UNLEASH THE BEAST
The Dodge Charger SRT Hellcat is a muscle car icon, engineered for adrenaline-pumping performance. Featuring a supercharged 6.2L HEMI® V8 engine that delivers a staggering 797 horsepower, the Hellcat is built to dominate both the track and the streets. Its aggressive design, advanced technology, and unparalleled power make every drive an unforgettable experience. With features like Brembo brakes, launch control, and a roaring exhaust note, the Charger SRT Hellcat is as bold as it is fast. Get ready to unleash the beast and experience the ultimate in American muscle.
SHELBY
GT: AN ICON REBORN
MUSCLE AND HERITAGE
The Shelby GT is a modern tribute to an American classic, delivering unmatched power, performance, and style. Equipped with a roaring V8 engine and precision engineering, the Shelby GT embodies the spirit of the open road. Its aggressive design, iconic racing stripes, and track-ready features make it a standout both on the street and at the track. Experience the legacy of Carroll Shelby with the Shelby GT—a true muscle car legend, reimagined for the modern era.
MCLAREN
P1 GTR: TRACK DOMINATOR
ULTIMATE PERFORMANCE
The McLaren P1 GTR is a hypercar crafted exclusively for the track, pushing the boundaries of engineering and performance. PowNew Cars drivetrain that combines a 3.8L twin-turbocharged V8 with an electric motor, it delivers an astonishing 986 horsepower, ensuring breathtaking speed and precision. With aerodynamic enhancements, lightweight construction, and a race-inspired cockpit, the P1 GTR is designed for unparalleled track domination. Its aggressive styling reflects its incredible capabilities, while advanced systems ensure maximum grip and control at high speeds. The McLaren P1 GTR is the ultimate expression of innovation, power, and exclusivity, reserved for the most passionate enthusiasts.
LAMBORGHINI
URUS: THE SUPER SUV
EXHILARATING PERFORMANCE
The Lamborghini Urus redefines what an SUV can be, combining the soul of a supercar with the versatility of a luxury SUV. Powered by a twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivering over 640 horsepower, the Urus accelerates with breathtaking speed while maintaining exceptional handling and control. With its bold design, luxurious interiors, and cutting-edge technology, the Urus offers an unmatched driving experience on any terrain. From city streets to rugged landscapes, the Lamborghini Urus is built for those who demand performance without compromise. Discover the ultimate expression of power and sophistication with the Lamborghini Urus.
